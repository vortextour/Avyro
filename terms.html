Platform Rules & Policies

Welcome to Avyro! Our platform is designed to reward you for building an active network. Please carefully read the operational rules and commission structures before you begin.

1 Account Registration & Activation

Creating an account on our platform is completely free. However, your account capabilities depend on its activation status.

  • Inactive Status: Newly created accounts are strictly in "Inactive" mode. In this mode, you cannot get your referral link, cannot refer others, and cannot withdraw funds.
  • How to Activate: You must upgrade to at least the "Bronze" plan by depositing the required fee. Once your deposit is reviewed and approved by the admin, your account status changes to Active.
  • Once Active, your dashboard features, Referral Link, and Referral Code will be fully unlocked.

2 3-Level Commission Structure

Our platform operates on a rewarding 3-level deep Multi-Level Marketing (MLM) network. When a user in your downline upgrades a plan, the fee is instantly distributed into three tiers:

Level 1 (Direct)

50%

Users who joined directly via your specific referral link or code.

Level 2 (Indirect)

10%

Users invited by your Level 1 members.

Level 3 (Indirect)

5%

Users invited by your Level 2 members.

* The remaining 35% of any transaction is strictly reserved for platform server maintenance and administrative profits.

3 Plan Upgrades & "Commission Skip" Rule

We offer 5 sequential plans: Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, and Diamond. This section outlines the most important rule of the platform.

The "Commission Skip" Rule

To receive a commission from a downline's upgrade, your own account must be on a plan that is equal to or higher than the plan they are purchasing.

  • Example of Winning: You are on the "Gold" plan. Your downline upgrades to the "Silver" or "Gold" plan. You will successfully receive the 50% commission.
  • Example of Losing (Skip): You are on the "Bronze" plan. Your downline decides to upgrade to the "Silver" plan. Because your level is lower than theirs, the commission will SKIP you entirely and go to the platform profit.
  • Advice: Always ensure you upgrade your own plan ahead of your active downlines to avoid losing massive commissions!

4 Withdrawal Policy

We are committed to providing smooth payouts for your hard work.

  • Withdrawal requests are strictly available for Active users only.
  • You must have a sufficient wallet balance to make a request.
  • When you submit a withdrawal, the funds are immediately deducted from your available balance and placed in a "Pending" state.
  • Our Admin team manually reviews and approves all requests to ensure security. Typical processing time is between 24 to 48 hours.
  • If you provide incorrect payment gateway details (wrong account numbers), Avyro holds zero liability for the lost funds. If a request is rejected due to invalid details, the funds will be automatically returned to your wallet.

5 Fair Play, Fraud Prevention & Bans

To maintain a profitable and sustainable ecosystem for all members, strict fair play rules are enforced globally.

  • No Self-Referral: You are absolutely forbidden from creating dummy accounts using your own referral link just to extract commission.
  • One Account Per User: Each individual is permitted to operate only one account. Our system tracks IP addresses and Device Fingerprints.
  • Zero Tolerance Policy: Any attempt to hack, manipulate the referral tree, or bypass the payment gateways will trigger an automated security lock.

Violation of these terms will result in an immediate, permanent ban. All wallet balances, downline networks, and pending withdrawals tied to a banned account will be instantly confiscated.
